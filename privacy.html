Cricket Manager 26 does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any of your data. Everything you do here stays on your own device. What data this game stores All progress — your squads, players, match history, finances, and settings — is saved using your browser's Local Storage, on your device only. This game has no server, no account system, and nothing you do here is ever sent anywhere. No collection of personal information This game does not ask for or store your name, email, location, or any other personal information. The "Manager Name" field is just flavor text kept in your own Local Storage. No tracking, analytics, or ads There are no analytics scripts, tracking pixels, cookies, or advertising in this game. No network requests The game runs entirely offline once loaded. It does not fetch data from, or send data to, any external server. Clearing your data You can permanently erase all saved progress at any time using "Reset Save Data" in Settings, or by clearing your browser's site data/Local Storage for this page. Third parties Since no data ever leaves your device, none of your information is available to the developer or any third party. Changes to this policy If this file is updated in the future, an updated policy would be included with it. Since the game works entirely offline, you control which version you're using.
